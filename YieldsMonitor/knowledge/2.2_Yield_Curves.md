# 2.2 Yield Curves Specification

## Purpose
Visualizes the shift and twist in the yield curves over the active time range.

## Snapshots
Two datasets are plotted for each curve (TIPS and Nominal):
- **Start of Range**: Dashed blue line representing yields at the very first point of the active range.
- **Latest**: Solid red line representing the most recent yields.

## Labeling
Datasets are labeled with the precise ET timestamp of the snapshot they represent.
